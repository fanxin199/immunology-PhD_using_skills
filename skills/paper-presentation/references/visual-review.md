# Visual Review Checklist

Use this checklist after rendering slides to PNGs.

## Readability
- Is every title fully visible?
- Are card headers fully visible?
- Is the body text large enough for projection?
- Is every figure still interpretable at slide scale?

## Layout
- Is anything clipped, overlapped, or jammed against the edge?
- Is the visual balance stable across slides?
- Are there any chips, callouts, or flow boxes that are too narrow for their text?
- Are dense figures large enough relative to the bullet text?

## Rendering
- Is Chinese text rendered correctly?
- Are fonts substituted in a way that changes spacing badly?
- Are figure labels or small legends too blurry to be useful?

## Final Output
- Re-render after every meaningful layout change.
- Build a montage for quick scanning before final delivery.
- Do not deliver after code-only checks; always inspect rendered output.
- After final delivery, delete intermediate text dumps, figure crops, render folders, montage files, and temp work directories unless the user explicitly asks to keep them.
