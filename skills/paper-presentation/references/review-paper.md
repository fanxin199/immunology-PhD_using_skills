# Review Or Perspective Deck Pattern

Use this reference when the PDF is a review, perspective, commentary, or methods paper rather than an original research paper.

## Default Structure
1. Title
2. Why this topic matters
3. Scope and thesis
4. Theme 1
5. Theme 2
6. Theme 3
7. Open questions
8. Take-home and discussion

## Rules
- Organize by ideas or themes, not by every figure.
- Use figures only when they clarify a model or summary better than text.
- Keep one central claim per slide.
- Prefer synthesis over detail dumping.
