# nsfc-grant

## Overview / 概览

`nsfc-grant` is a Chinese-first writing and revision skill for National Natural Science Foundation of China proposals.

`nsfc-grant` 面向国自然申请书撰写与深度修改，强调模块化推进、逐段修订、评审逻辑对齐，以及在不篡改原始科研数据的前提下优化表达与结构。

## Included / 包含内容

- `SKILL.md` describing the writing workflow
- `templates/` for rationale, research content, methodology, innovations, outcomes, and review criteria
- `examples/` and `references/` for reusable writing material
- `immunology/` notes for domain-specific idea framing
- `scripts/nih_reporter_search.py` for topic scouting

## Best For / 适用场景

- Drafting new NSFC proposals from scratch
- Restructuring an existing proposal section by section
- Keeping proposal writing aligned with reviewer logic and common scoring expectations

## Notes / 说明

This is one of the most domain-specific skills in the repository and is intentionally preserved in Chinese-first form.
